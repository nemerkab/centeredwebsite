Founder-in-Residence (FIR) Program

6-month equity-free residency for social enterprise founders building innovative software and technology solutions

Program Overview

The Impact Studio FIR Program is an intensive, 6-month, equity-free residency designed specifically for early-stage social enterprise founders building innovative solutions with software or technology components. The program is structured to nurture founders from the idea stage through to MVP and early market validation, with a strong emphasis on mentorship, community, and practical resources.

Duration: 6 months (hybrid model: in-person & virtual)

Program Structure

Month 1-2: Ideation and Validation

  • Intensive workshops on ideation, market research, and validation methods
  • Weekly 1-on-1 mentorship sessions to refine concepts
  • Peer cohort discussions to share insights and early findings

Month 3-4: Product Development and MVP Creation

  • Hands-on workshops in product design, UX/UI, and technology development
  • Weekly access to expert product and engineering mentors
  • AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure credits provided to reduce tech costs

Month 5: Impact Measurement and Storytelling

  • Dedicated sessions on developing an impact framework and metrics
  • Storytelling workshops to effectively communicate the mission and impact
  • Individual pitch coaching to prepare for investor presentations

Month 6: Fundraising and Go-to-Market Strategy

  • Investor readiness bootcamp, including intros to impact investors
  • Demo Day event where founders pitch to a curated audience of investors, industry leaders, and partners
  • Post-program action planning for fundraising, scaling, and growth

Participant Benefits

  • Personalized Mentorship: Direct guidance from successful social impact and tech entrepreneurs
  • Hands-On Workshops: Tailored sessions covering ideation, product development, impact measurement, storytelling, fundraising, and more
  • Essential Startup Tools: Free access to JoinSecret.com for discounts and credits on critical startup resources
  • SURGE Membership: Comprehensive support including coworking space, legal and financial consultations, mentor networks, and exclusive startup programming
  • Visibility & Recognition: Public acknowledgment of your Founder-in-Residence role at Centered, enhancing your professional credibility
  • Coworking Space: Free coworking and event space access during residency
  • Networking: Connection to a vibrant community of entrepreneurs, alumni, investors, and industry experts
  • Support Services: Pro bono legal, marketing, and design consultations

Program Commitment

  • 5 hours per week participating in structured Centered programming
  • Total of approximately 15 hours per week developing yourself and your venture
  • Full engagement in mentorship and peer cohort activities
  • Regular reporting and reflection on milestones and progress
  • Active contribution to the Impact Studio community, fostering peer support and collaboration

Who Should Apply

We're looking for bold founders ready to challenge the status quo and rebuild broken systems. Ideal applicants are:

  • Disruptors of Legacy Industries: Founders reimagining sectors like insurance, higher education, healthcare, housing, or other entrenched systems ripe for change.
  • Domain Experts: Individuals with deep professional or lived experience in their industry who have unique insights into inefficiencies and unmet needs.
  • Sell-First Mindset: Founders who validate ideas through real customer commitments before building — using pre-sales, pilots, or letters of intent to prove traction early.
  • B2B and Tech-Enabled Founders: Builders creating business-to-business or software-enabled service models that solve critical operational problems at scale.
  • Mission-Driven Innovators: Founders passionate about creating both economic and social value through systemic improvement.
  • Early-Stage Founders: Builders in the idea-to-MVP stage who are ready to commit time and energy to testing, validating, and launching their ventures.

Preferred Backgrounds / Experiences

  • Prior experience working inside a legacy industry (as an operator, consultant, or domain specialist)
  • Proven ability to sell or validate concepts before building — evidence of customer discovery, pilot programs, or pre-sales traction preferred
  • Exposure to startup or innovation ecosystems where rapid experimentation and validation were key
  • Strong analytical and systems-thinking mindset; ability to design scalable, tech-enabled solutions
  • Passion for purpose-driven innovation and learning through mentorship and community

What You'll Achieve

  • Validated and actionable MVP
  • Refined business strategy with clearly defined impact metrics
  • Investor-ready pitch deck and fundraising roadmap
  • Ongoing support from an engaged community of innovators and mentors

Outcomes and Long-term Support

Graduates leave the program with an actionable MVP, refined business model, clearly defined impact metrics, and investor-ready pitch deck.

Long-term access to alumni network, ongoing investor introductions, and community events.
