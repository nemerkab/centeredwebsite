Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 23, 2025

Centered (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides a private community and communication platform designed for founders. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Centered mobile app and related services (“Services”).

By using Centered, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the community and provide support.

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: name, email address, password, and profile details.
  • Messages: direct messages, channel posts, and other communications you send within the app.
  • Booking Information: session bookings, availability, and call preferences.
  • Tier & Membership Info: your membership level and related settings.
  • Support Requests: information you share when contacting our team.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: actions taken in the app (e.g., opening channels, sending messages).
  • Device Information: device model, operating system, app version.
  • Push Notification Tokens: used to send you alerts and messages.
  • Log Data: IP address, timestamps, and performance logs.

We do not sell or rent any of this data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the Centered platform
  • Enable messaging, channels, and bookings
  • Personalize your experience
  • Manage membership tiers
  • Communicate with you (including push notifications)
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Provide customer support
  • Maintain the security of the platform

We do not use your content to train external AI models.

3. How Your Information Is Shared

We only share your information in the following cases:

3.1 With Other Members

Depending on your activity and settings:

  • Your profile (name, photo, bio) may be visible to other members
  • Messages you send in public or private channels are visible to channel participants
  • Direct messages are only visible to the people in the conversation

3.2 With Service Providers

We work with third-party services that help us operate the app, such as:

  • Hosting providers
  • Analytics tools
  • Payment processors (if applicable)
  • Push notification services

These providers are only permitted to use data as needed to support Centered.

3.3 If Required by Law

We may disclose information if legally obligated to do so or to protect the safety of our users.

We never sell, rent, or trade personal information.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as long as needed to provide the Services. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time (see Section 8).

5. Security

We use reasonable physical, administrative, and technical safeguards to protect your information.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children’s Privacy

Centered is not intended for individuals under 16 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. International Users

If you access Centered from outside the United States, your information may be stored or processed in the U.S. or other countries where our service providers operate.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Update or correct your information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain notifications
  • Export a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at software@centered.team.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our product evolves.

When we do, we will update the “Effective Date” above.

We recommend reviewing the policy periodically.
