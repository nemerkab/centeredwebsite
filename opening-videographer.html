Videographer & Editor (Ad-Hoc, Local)

At Centered, we rapidly build tech startups and create playbooks for launching and scaling companies. Part of that work is creating content — both in-person and online — that supports founders, showcases startups, and shares what we’re learning along the way.

We’re looking for a Videographer & Editor who can jump in on an ad-hoc basis to capture and produce content. You’ll need to be local to Holland, Michigan and available to drop in on short notice when opportunities pop up.

This is a great fit for someone young, hungry, and looking to grow their skills and portfolio while working alongside a team building real startups.

What you’ll do

  • Capture in-person content at The Next Center and other local spots
  • Shoot quick founder interviews, event recaps, and behind-the-scenes startup moments
  • Edit short-form videos for web and social channels
  • Occasionally support online content creation (screen recordings, virtual events, etc.)

Who we’re looking for

  • Comfortable shooting and editing video with quick turnaround
  • Flexible schedule and ability to be on-site in Holland, often on short notice
  • Creative eye, but also practical — able to deliver content that’s fast, clean, and usable
  • Someone motivated to learn, grow, and build a strong portfolio of work

Engagement Details

  • Type: Contractor, ad-hoc as needed
  • Location: Must be based in or near Holland, Michigan
  • Hours/Pay: Project-based; details discussed per shoot/edit
  • Timeline: Work is ongoing, starting immediately

How to Apply

Email cfa@centered.team with a few samples of your video work and a short note about why you’re interested in working with startups.

If you don’t have a big portfolio yet, send something scrappy you’ve made — we’re looking for resourcefulness and hunger, not perfection.
