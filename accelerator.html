What Is the Customer Funded Startup Accelerator?

A fast-paced, sales-first startup program built for West Michigan founders who want to validate ideas, presell offers, and build real traction — not just pitch decks. Over 8 weeks you'll learn to:

  • Craft offers that resonate and convert
  • Establish pricing that funds your business growth
  • Validate customer pain points with paid presales
  • Launch three sales sprints with clear targets
  • Build a repeatable playbook for early revenue

Not every startup needs venture capital, but every startup needs to sell

Who It's For

  • Location: West Michigan–based founders or founding teams
  • Stage: Idea-stage, bootstrapped, or pre-seed ventures
  • Mindset: Action-oriented builders ready to test, sell, and iterate

What's Included

  • ✅ 8 Weeks of Expert-Led Sales Training
  • ✅ 3 High-Velocity Sales Sprints with Real Revenue Goals
  • ✅ Weekly 1:1 Coaching & Personalized Growth Plan
  • ✅ Stripe Setup + CRM Tools To Collect Payments Fast
  • ✅ Founder Video Content For Sales & Investor Outreach
  • ✅ Final Showcase To Sponsors & Local Business Leaders

100% Focused on Customer Revenue.

Founder Outcomes

  • Identified and validated a core customer pain point
  • Launched a pre-sellable offer and refined your sales messaging
  • Gathered feedback from 10+ paying early users
  • Built payment infrastructure and made real revenue
  • A repeatable early sales system & pitch-ready video content
