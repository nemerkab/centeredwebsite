Curated, small events designed to radically support founders. We bring founders together with service providers and investors to force collisions, spark meaningful connections, and enjoy a great meal.

The Idea

What would radically supporting a select number of startups look like? What if we got service providers and investors in the same room with startups that could benefit from their capital and services?

Michigan has terrific founders, ideas, investors, talent, and service providers, but they don't always find each other. Perhaps it's a density problem and reducing the space will create more collisions.

We proposed a one night event to bring together different aspects of our ecosystem into one room and sit around one table under the premise of doing what we can to support these startups.

What are Startup Dinners?

Startup Dinners are intimate, high-value networking events where real relationships are built. Unlike large networking events where connections are fleeting, our dinners create an environment where founders can have deep, meaningful conversations with the people who can actually help them grow.
