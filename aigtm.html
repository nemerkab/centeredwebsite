AI Automation Intern (Part-Time)

At Centered, we rapidly build tech startups and create playbooks for launching and scaling companies. We move quickly, test relentlessly, and share what works so founders and teams can go further, faster.

We're looking for a part-time AI Automation Intern to help us explore how AI can sharpen go-to-market practices. This role is about learning by doing: moving quickly, testing ideas, and not being afraid to try something new.

What You'll Explore

  • What would it look like to have an automation that creates hundreds of video ad variations and runs experiments?
  • How could AI handle outreach, testing dozens of messages across different customer segments in minutes?
  • What happens when you chain together multiple tools into a workflow that runs overnight while you sleep?
  • Where can automation make marketing not only faster, but smarter and more sustainable?

What We're Looking For

  • Curiosity about AI and a willingness to dive in, even if you don't know everything yet.
  • Comfort with moving fast—testing, failing, and trying again.
  • Clear communication and a collaborative mindset.
  • If you open X, Reddit, and other sources every morning to see the latest AI tools and tricks, you may be a fit for this role.

Details

  • Part-time: 10–20 hours per week
  • Compensation: $20/hour
  • Location: On-site most days at The Next Center in Holland, Michigan — you'll be surrounded by our team, other startups, and plenty of support resources.
  • Timeline: Fall 2025 start, with the opportunity to extend beyond the year

How to Apply
